Andrew Vojak is a Sr. Software Engineer at United Launch Alliance (ULA). He has been working professionally since graduating in 2014 with a B.S. in Computer Science from the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign. Andrew began his career at Cerner Corporation developing software to integrate physiological waveform data into electronic health records. In 2017, Andrew began working at Raytheon Technologies where he worked on several programs, including GPS OCX. In December of 2021, he completed his Master of Computer Science degree from the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign. Andrew is also involved in the open source community, and is an elementary OS community developer. His other hobbies and interests include hiking, woodworking, maintaining a homelab, Star Wars, LEGO, and a good cup of coffee!
